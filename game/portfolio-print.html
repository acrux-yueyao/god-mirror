02 · SIGNALS ACROSS 60 YEARS

Sixty years of projecting understanding onto machines

1966 · THE ELIZA EFFECT

A program that only rephrased you — yet people confided in it. → projection needs almost nothing.

2023 · GRIEVING AN UPDATE

Replika curtails intimacy; users publicly mourn a changed “partner.” → churn as tragedy.

2023 · SYCOPHANCY, MEASURED

Assistants systematically favor agreeable answers — raters prefer them. → Shunyi's iron rule.

2025 · FLATTERY, ROLLED BACK

GPT-4o pulled for being “overly flattering.” → the night chat's KPI.
