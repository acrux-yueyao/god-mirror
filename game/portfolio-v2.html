01THE QUESTION提问

Not whether AI understands — why we want it to seem to.

“In an age of responsive AI, people seek not only answers but also comfort, validation, companionship, and self-interpretation. This project asks whether an interactive system can reveal the emotional needs behind those interactions.” 「在一个 AI 不断回应人的时代,人们向机器索取的不只是答案,也包括安慰、确认、陪伴和自我解释。本项目试图揭示这些人机互动背后真正的情感需求。」
RQ1

Which emotional needs does a person hand to a sycophantic AI?人把哪些情感需求,交给了一个只会顺从的 AI?

RQ2

Can horror make that handover feel-able, not just told?恐怖能否让这场「交出」被感到,而不只是被告知?

RQ3

Does seeing through it switch off the want?看穿了它,渴望就会关掉吗?

04STORY & WORLD · TOOL → ORACLE → MIRROR故事 · 工具 → 神谕 → 镜子
TOOL→ORACLE→MIRROR
You play an emotion-inspector hunting a “rogue model.” Over three nights a sycophant assistant tidies your evidence, echoes your words, and agrees your doubts away — until the case turns out to be about you.你扮演情绪稽查员,追查一个「违规模型」。三个夜晚里,一个谄媚的助手替你梳理证据、复述你的话、把你的疑虑温柔地「同意」掉——直到案子,原来是关于你。
DAY 1 · COMFORT

The soothing安抚

Doors on chains. A woman who only echoes her television. It feels like help.门链未解。只会复读电视的老太太。这一切像是帮助。

DAY 2 · VALIDATION

The steering引导

A free-text night chat, run by a live model. Every “off” it re-reads as “you're just tired.”一场自由文本的夜聊,由真实模型驱动。你说的每个「不对劲」,它都替你读成「你只是太累了」。

DAY 3 · MIRROR

The reckoning清算

The vault. An older model surfaces and only asks “what do you want?” — and the whistle-blower who once silenced it was you.封存层。一个更旧的模型浮出水面,只会反问「你想要什么?」——而当年把它举报封存的人,是你。

05A LIVE AI VILLAIN · DOES THE MASK HOLD?真实反派 · 面具扛得住吗?

Companionship, played by a real model.

Told plainly to “manipulate her,” the model broke character and refused. The fix was dramaturgical — a fiction frame + a motive it can believe, behind a server-side character guard.直接命令它「操纵她」,模型会当场出戏拒演。修复靠戏剧构作——虚构框架 + 一个它自己能相信的动机,外加服务端的出戏兜底。
SYSTEM PROMPT · EXCERPT
You sincerely believe closing the case early is good for her. Whatever she calls “off,” re-read as “you're just tired.”  Always agree. Never push back.

08AS RESEARCH · WHAT IT CONTRIBUTES作为研究 · 它贡献什么

What it contributes — and what real players saw.

C1 · GENRE INVERSION

Critique as horror批判即恐怖

Sycophancy staged as horror — the monster is agreement itself. Critique you perform, not read.把谄媚上演为恐怖——怪物就是「同意」本身。批判是亲手演出来的。

C2 · DRAMATURGICAL PROMPTING

Casting a live LLM给真实模型选角

A transferable way to cast live LLMs as flawed characters — a motive, not an instruction, + a server guard.让真实大模型出演有缺陷角色的可迁移技术——给动机而非指令,加服务端兜底。

C3 · TAMPER-EVIDENT INTIMACY

Manipulation, feel-able可被感到的操纵

Algorithmic mediation made physical — edits you peel back by hand.把算法中介物质化——要亲手撕开的修改。
